About Our Club

Who We Are

The Channelview High School Robotics Club is a student-run organization dedicated to inspiring innovation and fostering STEM education through hands-on robotics competitions.

Our Mission

We aim to develop technical skills, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities while competing in the FTC (FIRST Tech Challenge) robotics competitions.

"The goal of the club is not just to build robots, but to build future engineers, scientists, and leaders who will drive technological innovation."
